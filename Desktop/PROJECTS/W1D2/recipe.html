Burger Recipe

This is the best goddamn burger recipe on the internet

This is the definition of a Burger!

Burger Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Meat Patties
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce

Directions

  1. Heat up a pan with oil.
  2. Cook patty on pan.
  3. Flip patty when one side is cooked.
  4. Put patty on buns.
  5. Throw cheese on buns
  6. Throw lettuce on buns.
  7. Throw out burger and order UberEats
